MISSION STATEMENT

At CJC Environmental Consulting we are dedicated to employing our knowledge in the Geographic Information Systems field to solving important environmental problems for our clients. Our skilled team members will provide you with quality mapping products using the most accurate and up-to-date data available.

ABOUT CJC CONSULTING

CJC Environmental Consulting was founded in November 2020 during the height of the global pandemic. During this time, many workers were laid off from their jobs. In the midst of this chaos Jessica Hobbins, Chelsea Penlington and Calvin Julie decided to improve their job security by learning the relevant skills to become GIS Specialists and open their own consulting business employing these highly sought-after skills. CJC Environmental Consulting specializes in mapping product development and is dedicated to delivering high quality products to their clients with the most relevant and up-to-date data.
